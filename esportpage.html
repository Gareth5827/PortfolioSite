I bring a great deal of energy, enthusiasm, and authenticity to everything I do! It doesn’t matter if it’s a stream, event, or resume, I always put my personality first and make sure everyone’s rocking a smile! I have done all kinds of casting, sponsor shoutouts, time filling, interviewing, and even a little bit of observing! Even if I’m not on the main broadcast, I have experience working events of varying sizes, from running game days by myself at my college campus, to working at big venues like The Aud.
Casting is something I have absolutely fallen in love with over the past couple years. Everything from finding creative ways to work in sponsors, talk about games people love, and bringing the energy to a broadcast with a co-caster is my bread and butter! I have casted with a few different organizations like Conestoga College, University of Waterloo, Tiffin University, Ora Esports, and many more to come! I have done great shows with casters of all experience levels, and while I usually flex between PBP and colour throughout a stream, I tend to let my co-caster tell me what they prefer and I play off of that. I am great at filling time, keeping people engaged, and making streams memorable!
When it comes to interviewing, I'm an allrounder that loves to make people laugh! I have done very technical interviews that dig into the minds of pro players, surface level interviews for players who aren't super comfortable on the mic, funny interviews where I get players to show their personalities, as well as serious interviews where players have come off a double loss and have them leave on a good note. I have talked with players and coaches of varying comfort levels both on and off stream, and typically I talk to players beforehand to let them tell me what I can and can't bring up during the interview. As a natural born people pleaser and class clown, there aren't many people I can't make smile or laugh when I talk to them!
Big events are where I thrive! From packed venues to main stage productions, I bring the energy that matches the scale of the moment.
Smaller events have their own charm. I love the intimate atmosphere where I can connect with everyone in the room and make each moment count.
